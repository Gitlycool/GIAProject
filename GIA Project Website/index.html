GIA Fund Raisers

GIA has many different partners, we host clothing drives, and fundraisers to support people in need

Clothing Drives

Drop off the clothes that don't fit you anymore to us, we can make sure that people in need will be able to receive those clothing. We host these clothing drives in school hours, and you can drop off the cloths at anytime.

Hackathons

We host hackathons that force of problems in the world that people are facing, we also theme our hackathons around issues so those issues can be solved

Fund-raisers

We fund-raiser for events that people don't know as well. For our fund-raisers we sell items or foods, and all profits go to foundations which makes sure that the money is going to people in need

If you want to join our hackathons, make sure that you have joined our Discord. That is where we post all the information about our upcoming hacks.

GIA Project, started as a club wanting to discuss policies, and as we discussed more about issues we wanted to do something about it so we create a non-profit org which helps people in need.

We will be posting about upcoming fund-raisers on our social medias, if you would like to donate contact us, or come to the fundraisers.
